My Favorite band

My favorite band is Hillsong United

Hillsong United is an Australian worship band. Christian rock and worship group Hillsong spun out of Australia's Hillsong Church, a Pentecostal ministry based in northwest Sydney. Founded in August 1983 by New Zealand natives Brian and Bobbie Houston, Hillsong Church was first known as Hills Christian Life Centre. At launch, the congregation counted just 45 members, but grew to 900 within four years. As the church's music ministry evolved, in 1986 the Houstons founded the Hillsong Conference, an annual event created to teach and nurture contemporary Christian musicians. I love their songs because not only do they bring peace and ambiance but also do they speak to my soul.

Joel Houston

Joel Timothy Houston is an Australian musician, songwriter, pastor, and leader in the Sydney-based band Hillsong United, a worship band of Hillsong Church. He was born on September 19, 1979 (41 years old) . His nickname is JTH He has been co-pastor at Hillsong NYC since 2010. He is the head of the Christian worship band Hillsong UNITED, for which he sings, plays guitar, and writes songs.

Brooke Ligertwood

Brooke Gabrielle Ligertwood (née Fraser, born 15 December 1983) better known by her stage name Brooke Fraser, is a New Zealand singer and songwriter best known for her hit single "Something in the Water", released in 2010. ... Her third studio album, Flags, was released in 2010 and is her most successful album to date. She is married to Scott Ligertwood with whom they have two daughters.

Taya Smith

Taya Smith is a Gospel Singer. She was born on May 10, 1989 (31 years old) in New South Wales. is an actress, known for The Wally Show Podcast (2013), Of Dirt and Grace, Live from the Land (2016) and Hillsong: Let Hope Rise (2016). She has been married to Ben Gaukrodger since March 23, 2018.

Aodhan King

Aodhan King was born on the 25th of December 1992, He was born in Sydney, Australia to an Indian mother and Irish father. He began attending Hillsong Church's youth ministry in 2004 before becoming a part of the creative team, and finally becoming a full-fledged staff member. He grew up with brothers, Niall and Brendy. He briefly dated fellow band member Taya Smith in 2014.

Marty Sampson

Martin W "Marty" Sampson is an Australian contemporary worship singer-songwriter, musician and worship leader at the Hillsong Church in Sydney. From the late 1990s he featured on the United Live albums and was one of the original Hillsong United band's worship leaders

Jad Willies

Jad was born and raised in New Zealand and at the age of 17 was leading the worship team in his local church. He was 21 when he moved to Sydney, Australia to pursue studies at Hillsong College and the harbour city quickly became home. Jad has been an integral part of the Hillsong UNITED team for over a decade. He is a songwriter and one of the most senior worship leaders on team. Jad is passionate about his calling to serve God within the Church and devoted to his family. Jad is married to Rebecca and together they have two children, George and Harriet.

Annie Garratt

American by birth, Annie Garratt came to Australia for Hillsong International Leadership College and has made Sydney her home for life. Originally traveling as part of UNITED, she is now one of Hillsong’s Creative Pastors, coaching worship leaders, training vocalists and speaks often about her love for Jesus. Passionate about building the church, Annie loves seeing people developed and released into their gifting. A gifted worship leader, she understands the healing, restoring power of God as we worship and loves seeing people transformed in His presence. Wife of Brent and mother of Madison, Hunter and Brooklyn, Annie is devoted to her family, her friends and her church.
